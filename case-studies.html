Logistics & Finance

Automating Accounts Payable for a Logistics Giant

A leading East African logistics firm was processing thousands of invoices manually. Their finance team spent over 100 hours per month on data entry, leading to errors and delayed payments.

We developed an IPA solution that uses AI to "read" unstructured PDF and email invoices, extract key data (amount, PO number, date), validate it against their ERP, and queue it for one-click approval.

Results:

  • 95% reduction in manual data entry.
  • 60% faster invoice processing cycle.
  • Full ROI achieved in just 8 months.
E-commerce & Retail

Personalizing E-commerce with a Custom Chatbot

A growing online fashion retailer struggled with high cart abandonment and generic customer support. Their team couldn't handle the volume of queries about sizing, stock, and returns.

We built a custom AI chatbot that integrates directly with their inventory system. It provides instant, 24/7 support, answers complex product questions, and offers personalized recommendations based on browsing history, recapturing lost sales.

Results:

  • 25% increase in average order value.
  • 70% of all support queries now handled by AI.
  • 15% improvement in customer retention.
